    Stages

    Brief

    This stage is to immerse yourself into the task
    as much as possible to form a concept based
    on marketing analytics.

    Analytics

    At the analytic stage, a thorough analysis of the
    market and competitors is carried out. The
    current task is to identify and to emphasise the
    weak parts of the market.

    Prototyping

    At this stage, each section is worked out
    separately and priorities are set.

    Concept

    At this stage, there is a transition from a
    graphic idea to its physical embodiment.
    Details are being worked out to achieve
    maximum efficiency.

    Design

    At the design stage, I work on detailing,
    defining fonts, colors and then adding
    decorative elements. The design takes on the
    final look.

    Adaptive

    Here I will adapt the design to all devices and
    check out if the project looks satisfying.

    Development

    After all the stages are completed, I
    assemble the design on Tilda. Or I prepare
    the project for a transfer to developpers.
